About Us

Our Aim

We are a team of passionate individuals who are committed to helping farmers save their crops from damage caused by wild animals. Our animal detection project is designed to detect and deter animals from entering crop fields using advanced technologies and machine learning algorithms. Crop damage caused by animals is one of the major threats in reducing the crop yield. Traditional methods followed by farmers are not that affective and it is not feasible to hire guards to keep an eye on crops. Since safety of both human and animal is equally vital, it is important to protect the crops from damage caused by animals and divert them without any harm. Our project is developed to help hundreds of farmers to protect their crops. We encourage you to explore our website and learn more about our animal detection project. If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us.

Our System

Our system uses advanced computer vision algorithms and machine learning techniques to detect and classify animals in real-time, providing accurate information about the presence and location of any animal threats. The system monitors entire farm through a camera which will be recording the surroundings and play appropriate sounds when animals are detected to drive them away from field. This allows farmers to take action quickly, preventing or minimizing crop damage and reducing the need for expensive and harmful chemical pest control methods. Also traditional methods for preventing the trespassing of animals is either by humans which is a common practice or shocked by electric fences where there is a risk of fire.
